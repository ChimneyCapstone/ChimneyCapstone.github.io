Welcome to Chimney

Technology is great. From the comfort of our homes, we can have nearly anything delivered, or ask for help with any task. But this convenience has a price. We’re more lonely than we’ve ever been and we’ve lost human connection with our community. We’re trying to change that. Chimney lets you use your phone to reconnect with your community by requesting goods and services from your neighbors, not strangers.

Get goods

Forget that final ingredient for tonight’s dinner recipe? Run out of paper towels? Or maybe you’re just ordering take-out? Let Chimney bring it to you!

Get services

Not all of us are handy around the house. Use Chimney to connect with the people in your neighborhood who have the skills you don’t.

Meet your neighbors

The benefits from knowing your neighbors are huge. From safety to having someone watch your cat while you’re on vacation, we’re all better off when we’re a part of our community.
