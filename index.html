Welcome to Chimney

Description

Technology is great. From the comfort of our homes, we can have nearly anything delivered, or ask for help with any task. But this convenience has a price. We’re more lonely than we’ve ever been and we’ve lost human connection with our community. We’re trying to change that. Chimney lets you use your phone to reconnect with your community by requesting goods and services from your neighbors, not strangers.
